
>"vehicle_size" 
is the vehicle size: length, width, height, by meters.

>"align.txt" 
is the lidar mounting config that generated by auto_align_tool automatically. The first 3 elements is the tanslation and the last 3 elements is the rotation relative to the standart vehicle coordinate (x points the front, y points the left and z points the sky with origin in the geometry center of the orthographic projection on the ground of the vehicle). Length unit is "meter" and the angle unit is radian.
